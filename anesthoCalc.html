AnesthoCalc

Download

Description


AnesthoCalc contains many simple tools, scores, classifications, useful for medical practitioners in anaesthesiology, emergency medicine and others.

This application was developed to simplify a way of calculating many important tools, formulas and scores for medical practitioners.

    ASA, Apgar, Apfel, Cormak-Lehane, GCS, HAN, Mallampati, RASS, SOFA, qSOFA, BMI, Body square, Basal metabolic rate, MET, Oxygen consumption, OSA STOP-Bang, Pressure converter, Concentration convertor, List of catheter sizes, Calculators of anesthesiological equipment, Units converter.



    MAP, SVR, PVR, QTc, max Heart Rate, CPP, BP in Heart cameras, normal hemodynamic PICCO values, Pulmonary Compliance, Vd/Vt, Anatomic Dead Space, Vital parameters for children in different ages, laboratory values, BGA, Acid-Base Disorders, Anion Gape..
